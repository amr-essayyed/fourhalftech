Questions

Before you hand over the phone

Will it sound like a robot to my patients?

No. It's tuned for warm, plain conversation, not scripted menus. Most callers move straight through their request without realizing they're not talking to a person — and if they ask, it says so honestly.

What happens with something urgent or clinical?

It never tries to make a clinical judgment call. Anything urgent, sensitive, or outside its scope gets flagged and routed straight to your staff, with the conversation history attached so nothing gets repeated.

Does it actually book into our real calendar?

Yes — it connects directly to your existing scheduling software, so it's always working off live availability. No separate calendar to manage, no double-bookings.

How long does setup take?

Most single-location practices are live within a day. We connect your calendar, load your insurance list and office policies, and run a short test batch of calls before it goes live for patients.

Can we turn it off for certain hours or lines?

Yes. You control which lines it answers and when — run it after-hours only, as overflow during busy stretches, or full-time across every line.
